About the Competition

A two-level inter-school art competition celebrating Sri Krishna Janmashtami — an easy and welcoming way for young artists to connect with our culture.

2-Level Structure

Level 1 is conducted within individual schools. Level 2, the inter-school final, is held at the ICC Temple.

Theme

All artwork is created around the theme “Sri Krishna at Vrindavan Village.”

Who Advances

Each school selects its top 3 coloring and top 3 painting students to advance to the inter-school final.

Categories & Eligibility

Category Eligibility Level 2 Selection Prize
Coloring 2nd to 5th Standard Top 3 students per school To be announced
Painting 6th to 10th Standard Top 3 students per school To be announced

3 final inter-school winners will be chosen in each category during the Level 2 competition and awarded prize cheques during the main Janmashtami cultural program.

How It Works

  1. School Onboarding

    Schools register and secure official permission to participate.

  2. Announcement & Engagement

    The competition is announced to students via posters and assemblies; participants register and receive their registration card.

  3. Level 1 — In School

    Held within each school in the second half of July, well before Independence Day.

  4. Shortlisting

    Each school selects its top 3 coloring and top 3 painting students to advance.

  5. Level 2 — Inter-school Final

    Held at 10:00 AM at the ICC Temple (Saturday, Aug 22 or 29) with kirtan, a short talk, Janmashtami invitations, light prasad and media coverage.

  6. Grand Finale

    Final winners are announced and awarded prize cheques during the main Janmashtami cultural program.

Registration

Registration is in two steps. Schools register first; then students within each school register for their category. Each registered student receives a registration card that doubles as the Level 1 hall ticket.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Students from 2nd to 5th Standard compete in Coloring; students from 6th to 10th Standard compete in Painting.

What is the theme?

All artwork is based on “Sri Krishna at Vrindavan Village.”

How does a school join?

A school coordinator fills the School Registration Form. Our team then visits to confirm permissions and schedule the Level 1 competition.

Is there a registration card?

Yes. Upon paying the participation fee, each student receives a registration card that also serves as the hall ticket for the Level 1 competition.

When and where is the final?

The Level 2 inter-school final is held at 10:00 AM at the ICC Temple on Saturday, Aug 22 or 29, 2026, before Janmashtami.
